Background

I'm a multidisciplinary designer who believes great design lives at the tension between logic and feeling. With over 4 years of experience, my work spans across product design, motion, and creative technology. I use tools like Figma, Framer, and TouchDesigner to bring ideas to life.

What drives me is the space between a design and its execution—the micro-interaction that makes something feel polished, or the accessible UI system that creates a meaningful impact for diverse users.
