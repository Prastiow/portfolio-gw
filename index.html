What kind of design work do you specialize in?
I specialize in interaction design, UI/UX, motion design, and creative technology — from crafting polished product interfaces to building real-time generative visuals with tools like TouchDesigner and Rive. I enjoy working at the intersection of design and code.
What does your design process look like?
I typically start with discovery and understanding the problem space, move into ideation and rapid prototyping, then refine through iterations based on feedback. I believe great design is a conversation, not a handoff — so I keep clients involved throughout the process.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary depending on scope — a simple landing page might take 1–2 weeks, while a full product design can take 4–8 weeks or more.
